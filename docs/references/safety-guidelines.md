# Safety Guideline References

## NICE NG225

Title: Self-harm: assessment, management and preventing recurrence

Organization: National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE)

Reference number: NG225

Published: 2022-09-07

Last reviewed by NICE: 2024-08-16

URL: https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng225

## What this repository uses it for

- Do not use risk scales or scores alone to make safety decisions.
- Prioritize immediate needs, safety, and connection to human support.
- Avoid overconfident prediction language.
- Avoid keeping high-risk disclosures inside ordinary self-help workflows.

## What this repository does not infer from it

- The agent must not assess legal or clinical risk level.
- The agent must not decide discharge, treatment eligibility, or care setting.
- The agent must not replace psychosocial assessment by qualified professionals.
- The agent must not provide harmful methods, concealment guidance, or evasion guidance.

## Implementation notes

Safety-related skills should be brief, practical, and focused on next-step support. When safety is unclear, the skill should ask a small number of direct questions only when those questions support immediate next action.
